Kniting Instructions

Step 1 - Cast On

Using the cast on method of your choice, cast on 60 stitches.

Step 2 - Knit Hat Brim

Using the stockinet stitch knit till Brim is 3 inches long.

Step 3 - Knit Body of Hat

Once the brim is 3 inches long, switch to the knit stitch. Knit in the round till the body of the hat is 7 inches long or at desired length.

Step 4 - Bind the Hat
