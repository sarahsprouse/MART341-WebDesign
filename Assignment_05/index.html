Harry Potter

J.K. Rowling

Harry Potter is a 7 book series that came out the year I was born. The series follows the lives of a young wizard and his friends as they navigate challenges in the wizarding world.

This series was magical to read because the charachters grew up as I did. My mom read all of the books out loud to my siblings and I. We all loved the books and movies. I remember staying up late and going to different book release parties and then staying up as late as I could to read the book after the party.

Harry Potter is a series loved by many and remains a comoforting read for me.
